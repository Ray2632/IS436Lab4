Undergraduate — Information Systems (IS)

Program overview

The IS program blends business, analytics, and systems development. Students learn requirements gathering, database design, and cloud integration.

Core courses

  • IS 101 — Introduction to Information Systems
  • IS 220 — Database Systems
  • IS 330 — Systems Analysis & Design
  • IS 410 — IT Strategy & Project Management

Sample degree plan

Year 1–2: foundations and electives. Year 3–4: advanced IS topics, capstone, internship.
